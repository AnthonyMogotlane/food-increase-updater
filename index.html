Food Increase Tracker

The year-on-year increase outstrips headline inflation, and even food inflation tracked by Stats SA. The latest Consumer Price Index for March 2022 shows that headline inflation is 5.9%, and food inflation was recorded at 6.7%. According to the PMBEJD, the cost of the household food basket continues to rise due to both global and local factors, with worse still to come. Therefore with food price checker we allow customers to keep in track with the food inflation so that they can be able to plan ahead for their budget.
